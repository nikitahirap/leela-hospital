LEELA EYE INSTITUTE LLP

LEELA EYE INSTITUTE LLP was inaugurated on 30th April 2006 at Iqbal Towers, Murbad Road, Kalyan.

In a bid to serve patients in a bigger and better way, the eye clinic shifted to a huge 3500 sq.ft premise at 1st floor of Patel Plaza in 2015.

LEI proudly inaugurated HAPPY EYES LASIK CENTER at same premises to provide complete eye care solutions under one roof.

About Doctors

DR. ABHIJIT WADEKAR

MS DNB, CORNEA SPECIALIST

Did his MBBS from Lokmanya Tilak Medical College, Sion.

Did his MS and DNB in Ophthalmology from INHS Ashwini, Colaba.

Later did fellowship in Cornea and Anterior Segment from L V Prasad Eye Institute, Hyderabad.

He has successfully done more than 500 Cornea Transplant Surgeries.

Dr. Abhijit Wadekar, who is a Super Specialist Cataract Surgeon in Kalyan has a strong track record of thousands of satisfied patients over the past 18 Years of his practice.
